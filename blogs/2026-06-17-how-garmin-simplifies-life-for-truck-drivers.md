A showcase of Garmin products for the 3.55 million commercial truck drivers in the US. Featured gear includes dezl wireless headsets (up to 60 hours of talk time), dezl OTR GPS truck navigators with custom routing, dezl DualView and BC 50 backup cameras, the Instinct 3 smartwatch with trucking features, and the subscription-free Garmin eLog electronic logging device for hours-of-service compliance.
