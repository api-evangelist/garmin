Garmin's global running data shows an average run distance of 4.82 miles, with younger runners maintaining faster paces while older athletes log longer durations. The analysis also reveals a 23% increase in runners who combine strength training with their running activities, reflecting a broader trend toward cross-training among endurance athletes. The data is drawn from Garmin Connect user activity across all regions.
